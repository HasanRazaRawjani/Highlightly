What it does

A local editor wrapped like a creator tool.

01

Frame

Builds a vertical canvas, blurs the full gameplay behind it, then centers the action in a clean 4:3 crop.

02

Hear

Finds every source audio stream, folds game, comms, and mic into a single transcript-ready track.

03

Caption

Uses word timing to group fast speech into readable caption bursts with bright mid-line emphasis.

04

Export

Renders finished MP4 shorts for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts with your preset styling already applied.
